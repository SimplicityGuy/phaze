<objective>
Wire each compute backend to its bound agent via a per-entry reference recorded at construction
(D-02), resolving `self.config.agent_ref` against `Agent.id` (D-01) — replacing
`ComputeAgentBackend.is_available`'s `select_active_agent(kind="compute")` single-active-compute
assumption. An absent/unregistered bound agent degrades to a hold (`is_available` False, never raises;
D-05), preserving the cron no-op discipline. Mirrors the Phase-70 MKUE-01 `KueueBackend._kube()`
record-don't-rederive pattern (the deliberate compute-side twin).

Purpose: Each compute entry resolves to ITS bound Agent, not "the single active compute agent" —
closing success criterion 2 and giving Phase 73 the per-agent binding it builds dispatch/push/reconcile on.
Output: an id-keyed liveness selector in enqueue_router + a fail-loud binding accessor + a rewired
is_available; the Plan-01 golden stays green.
</objective>
