<objective>
Define the three CONTRACTS the rest of Phase 73 consumes and wire the dispatch-side destination stamp. This is the interface-first plan: (1) `ComputeBackend` gains a push host, (2) `PushFilePayload` gains the per-file destination fields, (3) `services/backends.py` gains the `resolve_compute_backend` inverse-lookup helper AND `ComputeAgentBackend.dispatch` stamps the destination onto the enqueued push (record-don't-rederive).

Purpose: Every downstream reader (fileserver rsync argv in Plan 02, `/pushed` + `/mismatch` callbacks in Plan 03) reads the RECORDED destination — this plan establishes what "recorded" means and where it is stamped. The verbatim precedent is Phase 70 `KueueBackend.dispatch` stamping `staging_bucket`.

Output: `ComputeBackend.push_host` (+ optional `ssh_user`), `PushFilePayload.dest_*`, `resolve_compute_backend()`, and a destination-carrying `ComputeAgentBackend.dispatch`.
</objective>
