<objective>
Collapse the `/upload-failed` and `/mismatch` spill writes onto the `cloud_job` sidecar as the single CAS
domain. This is the phase's security deliverable (SC#2): `report_upload_failed` gains a CAS guard anchored
on `cloud_job.status` (D-09) with a FULL no-op on miss (D-10), a `pg_advisory_xact_lock` on its attempt RMW
(D-11), and the spill re-stamps to `status='awaiting'` instead of `FAILED` (D-03). `report_push_mismatch`'s
spill re-stamp changes identically (D-03).

Purpose: A late/duplicate/spoofed `/upload-failed` on an already-advanced file must not clobber it back to
`AWAITING_CLOUD` (T-83-01), and two concurrent `/upload-failed` must not lose an attempt increment
(T-83-02). Per-endpoint kind-specific CAS (D-12) avoids the rejected universal predicate.

Output: rewritten `report_upload_failed`; re-stamped `report_push_mismatch` spill; SC#2 CAS-no-op +
D-11 concurrency regression tests.
</objective>
