helpers already take a `KubeConfig`; only the public verbs still resolve it globally â€” lift that up.

`/pushed` callback the moment a 2nd non-local backend is configured.

its own auth, `backend_id`-scoped reconcile, and per-cluster reachability probe.
Output: bucket-of-globals `active_kube` retired, per-backend kube client, token hack gone, all kube
callers threaded, controller startup probing every cluster, companion scratch fix.
